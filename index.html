Empowering DC Women In Tech

DCFemTech is a coalition of women leaders aimed at amplifying women in tech organizations, sharing resources, and bringing leaders together to close the gender gap. Bimonthly meetings are inclusive and open to any women in tech leaders. Members are affiliated with organizations with women attendance that is 50% or higher who have a focus on technology have a primary focus in the mission statement about gender or women.
