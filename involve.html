Get Involved

Sponsor us!

DCFemTech is a registered 503c organization under Code for Progress. Sponsorships can include in-kind space donation, a charitable contribution of funds, or sponsorhips of our events: DCFemTech Awards, Tour De Code, Hack for Good, and more! To learn more about supporting DCFemTech or to become a sponsor please email us!

Become a member organization

To qualify to become a member organization, your group must:

  1. have women attendance that is 50% or higher
  2. have a focus on technology
  3. have a primary focus in the mission statement about gender or women.

For any questions or to become a member please email us!
