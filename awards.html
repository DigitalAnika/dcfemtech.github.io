The 2018
DCFemTech
Awards

Celebrating Power Women in

Code, Design & Data

The DCFemTech Awards will recognize Power Women in Code, Design, and Data based in the Washington, DC region. Nominated by their community, these women are working in the trenches of tech to help their company or organization achieve success, sometimes entirely behind the scenes.

Nominations are now open!

Know an amazing woman who codes, designs or works with data? Are you one? Nominate yourself or others for the DCFemTech Awards.

Nominees should be women in the metro DC area who code, design, or manipulate data on a regular basis.

Nominations are simple and easy to submit. Recipients will be announced in late April, so go ahead and nominate your awesome self or that amazing woman who should be recognized and applauded!

NEW FOR 2018: We want to share the love. We're creating an opportunity for a fresh crop of power women to receive recognition in 2018. Recipients from 2017 are not eligible for this year's award. Don't fret! You'll be able to nab that award again in 2019.

Nominations due by March 21, 2018. Questions? Email dcfemtech@gmail.com.
