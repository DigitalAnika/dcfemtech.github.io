DCFT Powerful Women Awards

Know an amazing woman programmer, designer or data scientist? Are you one? Nominate yourself or others for the DCFemTech Awards.

Nominees should be women engineers, designers or data scientists. Nominations are simple and easy to submit. Winners will be announced in mid-April, so go ahead and nominate your awesome self or that amazing woman you work with who should be recognized and applauded!

Nominations due by March 24, 2017.
