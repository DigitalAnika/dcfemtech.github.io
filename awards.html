DCFT Powerful Women Awards

May 18, 2017 | The Washington Post, 1301 K St. NW, Washington DC | 6pm - 8pm

The DCFemTech Awards will recognize powerful women programmers, designers, and data scientists based in the Washington, DC region. We'll congratulate women who are working in the trenches of tech to help their company or organization achieve success, sometimes entirely behind the scenes.

Nominations now closed. Stay tuned for details on award recipients and event details! Follow @dcfemtech on Twitter for the latest.
