DCFT Powerful Women Awards

Know an amazing woman coder or designer? Are you one? Nominate yourself or others for the DCFemTech Awards.

Nominees should be women engineers or developers in metro DC who code OR design on a regular basis. Nominations are simple and easy to submit. Winners will be announced in mid-April, so go ahead and nominate your awesome self or that amazing woman dev who should be recognized and applauded!

Nominations due by March 17, 2017.
